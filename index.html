About Me

Hi! My name is John Lenkelt Sijera, a fresh graduate senior high school STEM student with honors from St. Anthony College of Roxas City. I'm on the lookout for an opportunity that will let me merge my passion for coding computer languages and kickstart a career as a Software Engineer. Working with a variety of personalities and finding answers to complex issues is a challenge I appreciate. I have a knack for being composed in a storm. The first computer language that I discovered and learned was HTML during the 8th grade, and that was the time I found coding amusing, and then I envisioned myself working in programming. Then during the 10th grade, I discovered Arduino when we were assigned to create a robotic thesis. In my team, I was the one who propose our project, which is titled "Project Aqua: An Arduino-based Smart Aquaponics System". However, I did not do all the coding because I was the one who mostly created the research paper, PPT presentation, budgeted, presented,and modeled our project, and scheduled work plans. I was astonished when I heard our thesis won the "Excellence Award in Agriculture". I strongly believe in the quote "Hard Work Always Pays Off".
